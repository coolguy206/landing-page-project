FAQs

Do I have to sign-up for the OpenSky Visa Gold Card?

The OpenSky Gold Card is currently available by
invitation to existing OpenSky Secured Card customers
only. This email is your invitation.

Am I already approved?

Yes, you are already approved. Clicking the button
above will take you to a landing page where we will
simply need to verify your personal details – that’s how
we know to send your new Visa Gold Card to
you. There
is no application.

Is there a credit check for this card?

No, there is absolutely no credit check when you sign
up for the OpenSky Gold Card.

Do I need to provide a security deposit for this card?

No. It is not a secured card. You will receive a brand
new card with it’s own credit limit, and it will be
reported to all three major credit bureaus as an
additional trade line.

Will this affect my credit score?

The OpenSky Visa Gold Card is the perfect companion
to your existing OpenSky Secured Card. It gives
you
more spending ability and flexibility to manage your
expenses, while continuing to build your
credit. Now
you can spend more and maintain the same utilization
rate. Your credit score is impacted
by various factors,
including, but not limited to, the length of your history,
the type and amount of
your credit lines, your
utilization, and your payment history.

  • What is the OpenSky Visa Gold Card?

    The OpenSky Visa Gold Credit Card is a new fully unsecured credit card, and is the perfect companion to your existing OpenSky Secured Card. It gives you more spending ability and flexibility to manage your expenses, while continuing to build your credit.

    Here are some great reasons to sign up for the new OpenSky Gold Card:

    • Fully unsecured credit line with no deposit required
    • More spending flexibility to manage your expenses
    • An additional trade line that is reported
    • Ability to continue to build your credit score through responsible use & behavior
    • No credit check
    • Reporting to all three credit bureaus
